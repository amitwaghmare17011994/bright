Bright Tutorial offers a two-year program for X passed students, aimed at building a strong foundation for securing admission in IITs. The course starts with the very basic fundamentals and gradually builds up on the difficulty level to meet the requirement of JEE Mains & Advance. It sets the students on a carefully structured path.

JEE Mains consist of 2 papers:

Paper 1 focusses on in B.E. or B.Tech courses and is conducted both online and offline. While, Paper 2, which is conducted offline, focusses on B. Arch. or B. Planning courses.

JEE Advanced consist of 2 papers:

JEE Advance is the examination which is conducted as a second round to JEE Mains, to secure admission at Indian institute of Technology (IIT), Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore and other such premier institutes. Only top 2 lakh students who qualify in the JEE Mains are eligible to appear for IIT-JEE Advance. While the test has no set pattern, it consists of 2 papers and each paper comprises of three sections: Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. This test checks the reasoning ability, comprehension skills and analytical power of the students
