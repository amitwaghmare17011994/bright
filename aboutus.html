About Us

Bright Tutorials is the most trusted coaching institute for IIT-JEE & Medical-NEET preparation in Rajgurunagar. It is being managed by a team of enthusiastic academicians who are committed to success of aspirants aiming towards IIT’s, IIIT’s and NIT’s. We support and nurture students, guide them thus ensuring that their dream get strong wings to fly. The teachers are always ready to share student’s problems and solve their queries. The teachers not only teach them but even play management games and enjoy with the students. At Bright Tutorials, we believe in harnessing the individual strength of every student. Our motive is that each student recognizes and develop their strengths, build upon it and achieve the desired success of career...

“Bright Tutorials” is brain child of Mrs. Neha S. Naikade, a young, energetic women entrepreneur and sound IT professional. She is currently acting as the chief mentor of Bright Careers & Bright Tutorials and guiding youngsters who wants to pursue their career in IT, Engineering and Medical field. She is MD & CEO of Sysnik IT Solutions which works in Core Banking Software Solutions and Enterprise Software Development sector. “Bright careers” understands the very pulse of rural youth. We gauge their potentials and provide tailor-made learning programmes that can bring out the best in them. At Bright Careers, the rural youth get just the comfort-zone that they need to trigger off their potential skills. We know how to shape them and hone them to boost their confidence.
